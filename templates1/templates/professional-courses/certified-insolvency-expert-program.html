About Certified Insolvency Expert Program

Certified Insolvency Expert Program (CIEP) offered by BSE Institute Ltd. is a 10 month online professional program. This online law course aims to create highly qualified legal professionals and is precisely created to concentrate on Indian corporate insolvency law which provides students with a complete understanding of its structure and functioning.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Graduate of any stream can apply.
  • Student should have graduated from a recognized university.
  • Working professionals having basic investment knowledge can also apply.

Certified Insolvency Expert Program is an online course with a duration of 10 months which a student can pursue after graduation. This course offers knowledge and expertise in an important legal segment- Insolvency Law.

Insolvency is undoubtedly the most preferred career option in law a professional can undertake. As this law domain is expanding, the career opportunities in it are diverse and include a wide range of job roles in the legal sector with a high pay scale.

The Certified Insolvency Expert Program is designed for those who want to pursue a career as an insolvency professional or who want to take up other roles in the value chain, in India, and in foreign jurisdictions. So, if you are a student who is dedicated to building a career in insolvency law, this course is ideal for you.

As a pioneer and recognized authority in legal education, BSE Institute focuses on creating competent professionals. The institute grooms young talent, trains them accordingly and prepares them for successful careers in their respective domains. It will be a wise step to complete your Insolvency Expert Program at BSE Institute Ltd. This online course uses a mix of interactive techniques, such as learning through live webinar sessions, assignments and case study oriented training. Through a unique mixture of theory, practical teaching methods and practitioner interactions, this program delivers the skills necessary to make the transition to an Insolvency Expert.
