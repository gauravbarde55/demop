About Post Graduate Diploma in Cyber Security

The Post Graduate Diploma in Cyber Security (PGDCS) is a one-year course provided by BSE Institute Ltd. in collaboration with Mumbai University's Garware Institute of Career Education and Development (GICED). With the growth of technology, security and privacy has become a global concern. Cyber Security deals with preventing unauthorized access to, damage to, or inaccessibility of computer systems. Through the Post Graduate Diploma in Cyber Security, the student will gain knowledge about strategies for safeguarding the infrastructure and securing data, including risk analysis and mitigation, cloud-based security, and compliance. Through both theoretical and interactive learning techniques, this course seeks to develop the student into a professional cybersecurity specialist.

The academic curriculum of this data science and analytics course is designed to enable students to gain an in-depth understanding of the key technologies used in cyber security, viz. IoT, Penetration Testing, Cloud security, etc. This course helps identify the various cyber compromising attacks and what one can utilize to assess and mitigate those attacks.

Eligibility Criteria for the PGDCS course:

  • Graduates with technical or science background from a recognized University with a minimum of 60% score.
  • Students awaiting final semester results of graduation.
  • Working professionals can also apply.
  • Candidate should be of or below 25 years of age.

Post Graduate Diploma in Cyber Security is an offline course and the lectures will be conducted at the BSE Institute Ltd. campus in Mumbai.

The duration of the Post Graduate Program in Cyber Security is 1 year and spans across 2 semesters.
