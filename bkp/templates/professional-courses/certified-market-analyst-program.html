About Certified Market Analyst Program

The Certified Market Analyst Program is an 80 hour online executive program aiming to develop proficiency in applying more advanced analytical techniques and technical analysis theory. This technical analysis course is designed to provide expertise on analysing stock market trends as well as predict trading movements using various technical analytic tools such as point and figure, line, and candlestick charts. This course is for technical and market analysts, which will open up opportunities in diverse roles in the fields of trading and finance.

The Certified Market Analyst Program is an online professional stock market and technical analysis course offered by BSE Institute Ltd. This comprehensive program provides an understanding of the working forces of the markets and helps the student gain exposure to a wide variety of technical tools, allowing them to learn what fits their trading style and temperament. The student acquires the ability to apply and analyse technical indicators for decision making purposes.

Market analysis is critical for organisations to better understand and serve their customers in order to develop and remain competitive. Marketing analysts research market circumstances to help organisations understand what sorts of products or services people desire, who will buy them, and at what price. In today's fast changing environment, talented and skilled market specialists are required to design successful campaigns and assess market trends in order to make profitable decisions.

The Certified Market Analyst programme is an 80-hour executive programme that aims to acquire competency in the application of more advanced analytical tools and technical analysis theories. It will entail rational and consistent research opinions, portfolio strategies, and trading judgements based on a variety of charts and technical data.
