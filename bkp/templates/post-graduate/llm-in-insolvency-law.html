About LL.M. in Insolvency law for Executives for Executives

LL.M. in Insolvency Law for Executives is a one-year online interactive course offered by BSE Institute Ltd. in collaboration with Maharashtra National Law University. The Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016 (IBC) is an Indian law that creates a consolidated framework that looks over insolvency and bankruptcy proceedings for individuals, companies, and partnership firms. This virtual learning course is precisely designed to focus on corporate insolvency law in India and a thorough understanding of its framework, including its procedures and processes.
