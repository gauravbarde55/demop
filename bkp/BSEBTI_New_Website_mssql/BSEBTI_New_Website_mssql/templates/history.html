History

The BSE Institute Ltd. is a 100% subsidiary of BSE Limited. BSE Institute Ltd. inherits the knowledge and insights into the capital markets industry garnered over the past 148 years by BSE. Our course design and development is firmly grounded in knowledge of business processes—in entrepreneurial companies and established firms unconstrained by the boundaries of academic disciplines.
For more than two decades, the BSE Institute Ltd. faculty have drawn on their passion for teaching, their experience in working with organizations worldwide, and the insights gained from their experience to educate professionals who have shaped the practice of business in financial markets not only in India but also in many other countries around the world.

Conducted specialized skill enhancement programs for the youths and students of Jammu and Kashmir.

Country’s Largest One-Time Skill Development Program for the Investment Banking Industry Completed

Created a supply chain of trained young graduates for the high tech Investment Banking Industry.

2018

Won the IPN Employability Award for Asia Pacific

BSE Institute Ltd. won the prestigious IPN Global Best Award for Youth Employability at the NASA centre in Houston, Texas for its pioneering work in this field.

Unique Start-up Incubator set up at IMS, BHU

Set up an start up Incubator at BHU in collaboration with Niti Aayog. This incubator was inaugurated and dedicated to the nation by Hon. Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi.

2018

Demonstrated Stock Trading as a Financial Skill Trade in the World Skills Championship

BSE Institute was the only professional institute from India to be invited to participate in and demonstrate stock trading as a skill at the World Skill Championship held at Abu Dhabi.

First Bachelors in Capital Markets Program Launched in the Country

Launched the first undergraduate degree program focussed on Capital Markets. Students on completion embark on a rewarding career in various organizations like banks, Stock Exchanges, broking houses, financial service companies, microfinance organizations, PE fund houses, and NBFC's etc.

Launched Dalal Street Lit Fest—a Unique Lit Fest Focussed on Books, Stories & Entertainment

Dalal Street Lit fest is the first of its kind and only literature festival to be hosted in a stock exchange. This lit fest is an exchange of words, thoughts, and expressions that amalgamate the two diverse worlds.

World's first start up accelerator housed within a stock exchange set up

World's first start-up accelerator housed in a stock exchange—a unique cross-border start-up accelerator set up with Ryerson University Toronto and Simon Fraser University, Vancouver Canada and India. Incubated close to 400 start-ups till date

Launched Training Programs on BFSI Skills to ensure employability for young Youths , Women in the multiple states

Graduation of the First Batch of MBA Students

2012

Launched the First National Level Stock Trading Competition—Stock Torero

The first national stock trading competition was conducted using real stock market software and environment, thereby simulating the market environment for college students.

2012

BSE Institute Expands its Horizons

Over the years, BSE Institute became one of the most sought-after educational institutes in the area of financial learning. In order to provide financial knowledge to students across India, we opened multiple centres all over the country.

2012

First Banking and Finance Program Launched

Launched a post-graduate program in Banking and Finance. This program aims to equip individuals to commence a career in Banking Industry

2011

BFSI Sector Skill Council of India set up as a nodal body to implement all National Skills program under NSDC

The BFSI Sector Skill Council of India, under NSDC (National Skill Development Corporation), was established as a not for profit. A nodal body across BFSI Industry and Government of India, it is responsible for implementing all the National Skills Program.

2011

Vocational Programs Launched

BSE Institute launched its first undergraduate, concurrent vocational program—GFMP. The Global Financial Markets Professional Program is meant for aspiring undergraduate students. Unique to the industry, it delivers first-hour, first-day job-ready financial markets skilled resources.

2011

World's first MBA in Capital Markets Introduced

BSE Institute launched the world's first MBA focused in Capital Markets.

2010

BSE Institute Becomes a Fully Owned Subsidiary of BSE

Set up as a fully owned 100% subsidiary of the Bombay Stock Exchange Ltd. BSE Institute continues to inherit the knowledge from BSE Ltd., which has been garnered over the past many years.

1989

BSE Training Institute Established

BSE Training Institute Ltd. was established as a department of BSE Ltd in the year of 1989 as a Center of excellence for Financial Literacy. Inaugurated by the then the Finance Minister of India, it was a first of its kind institution across South - East Asia, focused to training brokers, government officials, Regulators, students amongst others.

The Institute focuses on developing skills for employability, education for developing talent, and Innovation driven by entrepreneurs who create jobs.
