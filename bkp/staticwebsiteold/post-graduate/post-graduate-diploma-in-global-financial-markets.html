About Post Graduate Diploma in Global Financial Markets

The Post Graduate Diploma in Global Financial Markets is a 2 year postgraduate course offered by BSE Institute Ltd. in collaboration with the University of Mumbai and the Garware Institute of Career Education and Development. The financial markets arena is a constantly evolving field in which updated and specialized knowledge is required. This course is designed to give students in-depth knowledge of Global Financial Markets as well as hands-on experience in the Financial Markets industry which will not only open up numerous job opportunities but also aid them in preparing for a wide range of careers in financial markets around the world.

This financial markets course begins with the very basics that form the core of finance, such as Financial Accounting, Business Statistics, Derivatives, Strategic Financial Management, and Financial Statements and Fundamental Analysis. It eventually goes into highly advanced, specialized fields of study like Forex & Debt Markets, Financial Modelling, Investment Banking, Treasury & Risk Management, and Securities Law.

BSc Finance is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study of financial management, investment analysis, financial markets, and financial systems. The course curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in finance principles and theories, accounting, economics, statistics, and mathematics. Students learn how to analyze financial data, make investment decisions, manage financial risks, and develop financial strategies for businesses and individuals.

BSc Finance offers a wide scope of career opportunities in the financial industry. Graduates can pursue a variety of roles in investment banking, asset management, financial analysis, corporate finance, risk management, financial planning, and more. They can work for large financial institutions, multinational corporations, government agencies, and consulting firms. The finance industry is a fast-paced, dynamic field that offers high potential for career growth and advancement. In today's economy, there is a growing demand for financial professionals who can navigate complex financial markets and help businesses and individuals make sound financial decisions. As such, the scope of studying BSc Finance is broad and offers a promising career path for those interested in finance.

Studying BSc Finance is a wise decision for several reasons. Firstly, the finance industry is growing fast and offers a variety of career opportunities. Secondly, graduates with a BSc Finance can earn a competitive salary in finance-related roles. Studying finance also provides practical skills for managing personal finances and strategic thinking skills to analyze complex financial data. The skills learned are transferable to other industries. Overall, studying BSc Finance offers valuable skills and knowledge for a fulfilling and lucrative career in the finance industry.
Studying BSc Finance from BSE Institute Ltd is an excellent choice due to its industry-aligned curriculum, practical-oriented approach, experienced faculty, strong industry connections, and robust placement opportunities. The institute also has world-class infrastructure, providing a comfortable and conducive learning environment. Overall, studying BSc Finance from BSE Institute Ltd offers a well-rounded education with practical experience and access to a wide range of career opportunities in the finance industry.

Admission Process for BSc Finance-

  • Fill the application form online Click here on HBSU University website or collect the form from our centre in Bombay Stock Exchange, Mumbai.
  • Appear for the entrance exam, where your basic competency will be tested and gauged. Get shortlisted for a personal interview round with our faculty.
  • On selection, acknowledge the offer letter and confirm your admission.

Eligibility Criteria for BSc Data Science course:

  • Candidate with IB Diploma is eligible only if he/she has offered Mathematics /Mathematics & Statistics at SL/HL.
  • Student should have 50% aggregate marks in any stream (Science, Commerce, Arts).
