About Post Graduate Program in Banking & Finance

The Post-graduation Program in Banking and Finance (PGPBF) by BSE Institute Ltd. is a one-year program that gives insight into the various aspects of banking and finance. The finance and banking industry is one of the fastest-growing sectors since it both directly and indirectly contributes to the global economy. In addition to teaching students about banking and finance, this course also covers economics, information technology, technical analysis, and wealth management. It seeks to mold the student into a knowledgeable professional prepared to work in the banking and financial industry using a combination of theory and practical ways of teaching by highly experienced faculties.

The PGPBF is a one-year course that offers detailed knowledge of banking and financial operations. The Post-Graduation program in Banking & Finance (PGPBF) aims to equip individuals to survive in a banking organization both in terms of technical and non-technical aspects. You will gain all-round knowledge of banking operations, FOREX markets, international trade and finance, bank credit, legal environment of banking, and IT systems in banking. The PGPBF is a unique program because of its subject coverage and highly experienced faculty from the banking sector.

Banking has become a niche career for seekers & enthusiasts in the field of commerce & accounts. As per the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), India’s banking sector is sufficiently capitalized & well-regulated. Credit, market & liquidity risk studies suggest that Indian banks are generally resilient and have withstood the global downturn well. The advancements in technology have brought the mobile and internet banking services to the fore. The banking sector is laying greater emphasis on providing improved services to their clients and also upgrading their technology infrastructure to enhance the customer’s overall experience.

Choosing to study banking and finance is a wise option for those interested to fast track their careers & wish to get exposure of global markets at an early stage. It is thus considered as the first step towards understanding the global economy, discovering the challenges of the marketplace & identifying themselves with tremendous opportunities, both within the country and abroad. Banking and finance is a logical choice if you want a career in a fast-paced and booming industry and a PG course in banking and finance will make you completely prepared to face the competitive corporate world. So, if you want to pursue a career in banking and finance PGPBF is ideal for you.

The Post Graduate Program in Banking and Finance by BSE Institute Ltd aims to develop future banking and financial professionals. In addition to teaching students about the practical aspects of banking and finance, this program also strengthens their technical and managerial abilities. BSE Institute, as a pioneer and established leader in Financial Education, focuses on developing talent for the BFSI industry. The BSE institute grooms young talent, trains them accordingly and prepares them for a successful career in the BFSI industry across multiple domains. Completing your Post Graduate Program in Banking & Finance at BSE Institute Ltd. will be an intelligent step toward starting your career in Banking or Financial Markets.

Admission Process for Post Graduate Program in Banking & Finance at BSE Institute Ltd.

  • Fill out the application form online at Click here
  • Appear for the aptitude test, where your basic competency will be tested and gauged.
  • Get shortlisted for a personal interview round.
  • Acknowledge the offer letter and confirm your admission

Eligibility Criteria for the PGPBF course:

  • Graduates from a recognized University with a minimum of 60% score.
  • Students awaiting final semester results of graduation.
  • Working professionals can also apply.
  • Candidate should be of or below 25 years of age.

Post Graduate Program in Banking & Finance is an offline course and the lectures will be conducted at the BSE Institute Ltd. campus in Mumbai.

The duration of the Post Graduate Program in Banking & Finance is 1 year spanning across 2 semesters.
