About Post Graduate Diploma in Regulatory Compliance Management

The Post Graduate Diploma in Regulatory Compliance Management is a one year online course offered by BSE Institute Ltd. and Maharashtra National Law University (MNLU). This program focuses on the key principles, frameworks, and practices related to regulatory compliance management in various industries. This provides students with a strong understanding of the regulatory landscape and its impact on organizations, as well as the tools and strategies needed to manage regulatory compliance effectively. Students will explore topics such as corporate governance compliance, compliance management, risk assessment, compliance auditing, international compliance management, compliance reporting, and compliance ethics. After completion of the course, students will be equipped with the skills and knowledge necessary to design, implement, and manage effective compliance programs within their organizations.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Graduates with minimum 60% from a recognized University.
  • Students awaiting final semester results of graduation can also submit their application by providing their Hall Ticket.

  • Acknowledge the offer letter and confirm your admission

Eligibility Criteria for the PGDCS course:

  • Graduates with technical or science background from a recognized University with a minimum of 60% score.
  • Students awaiting final semester results of graduation.
  • Working professionals can also apply.
  • Candidate should be of or below 25 years of age.
