About Global Data Science Program

The Global Data Science Program (GDSP) by BSE Institute Ltd. is a two year vocational data science program engineered to develop global data science expertise among students with its curriculum covering data science and analytics fundamentals and its substructures such as Machine Learning, Big data, R programming, Hadoop, etc. The data science program is developed by industry maestros and aims to inculcate optimal data science mastery among students seeking to pursue a career in the domain, along with 100% placement assistance.

The Global Data Science Program (GDSP) by BSE Institute Ltd is a vocational data science programme designed to develop global data science expertise in students. Its curriculum covers data science and analytics fundamentals as well as substructures such as Machine Learning, Big Data, R programming, Hadoop, and others. The data science programme was created by industry experts with the goal of instilling optimal data science competence in students wishing to pursue a career in the subject, as well as providing 100% placement support.

Data science is one of the most in-demand occupations today, with data science technologies fast expanding and data becoming increasingly valuable. As data becomes more important for businesses, the work of a data scientist will become even more important. More data scientists will be needed to make sense of data as it becomes larger, more complicated, and more accessible through big data analytics technologies. This broadens the scope of data science in the future and makes it an extremely lucrative employment option.

Global Data Science Program (GDSP) guides the students about the statistical and scientific methods for data analysis through experiential practicable exercises. This program will be delivered by our data science experts and will completely focus on real-world business scenarios. This course provides in-depth knowledge on the most in-demand areas in Data Science like Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence by applying experience in utilizing essential tools and technologies such as Python, R, Tableau, and Machine Learning. You will learn about complexities of data interpretation, master technologies like Machine Learning, and master powerful programming abilities.

The GDSP program from BSE Institute Ltd offers a state of the art curriculum covering the vast lengths and breadths of data science making sure the student gains optimal understanding of various data science concepts. The curriculum also comprises of Industry Practice in form of internship at leading organizations from the industry. This added advantage of Industry Engagement makes it an even more viable option for anyone who wishes to build his / her career in this Industry. The program ensures that participants master the skills they learn before they begin their professional careers.

Students who have completed their 12th from any stream from a recognized Board are eligible for the Global Data Science Program.

The Global Data Science Program is an offline classroom course conducted in the BSE institute Ltd campus in Mumbai.

The Global Data Science Program is conducted over the period of 2 years and spans over 4 semesters and 1 semester of industry practice.
