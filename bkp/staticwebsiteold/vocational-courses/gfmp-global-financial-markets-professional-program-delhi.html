About Global Financial Markets Professional Program

Global Financial Market Professional program offered by BSE Institute Ltd is a comprehensive 10 months program covering the lengths and breadths of financial markets and its intermediaries such as Capital Markets, Investment Banking, Mutual Funds, Securities, etc. The course is curated by seasoned market experts for students who are keen on becoming the next financial market pro.

The academic curriculum of this global financial market course begins with understanding the fundamentals of financial markets, derivatives, fintech, mutual funds and dives deep into organizational activities such as financial modelling, business data analytics, financial reporting. A key highlight of this financial market course is that it provides 6 months of industry practice and 100% placement assistance after completion of the course.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students from any background who have completed their 12th or above can apply.
  • Student should have completed their education from a recognized Board/University.

The Global Financial Market Professional program is a comprehensive vocational financial market course provided by BSE Institute Ltd covering the vast domain of financial markets, its function and it's intermediaries such as Capital Markets, Investment Banking, Mutual Funds, Securities, etc.

Financial markets are poised to become the industry with the most job creation potential. The exponential growth of financial markets has given rise to a plethora of companies and occupations that require a diverse set of skill sets and expertise. Experts believe that, after information technology, the financial industry will be the next big thing in terms of job creation and self-employment opportunities.

The Global Financial Market Professional programme, with a concentration on financial markets, is a professional degree programme that intends to educate students for professions in finance and related sectors. It covers a wide range of topics such as financial management, technical analysis, corporate finance, and financial modelling. This global financial market course provides students with a thorough understanding of financial ideas, theories, and practises that may be utilised in a number of business and financial situations.

BSE Institute, as a pioneer and established leader in Financial Education, focuses on developing talent for the BFSI industry. The Global Financial Markets Professional Program is the best course for someone seeking to forge a career in the financial markets. And where else could one learn about financial market, than the financial stomp ground of India itself - Bombay Stock Exchange Limited. BSE Institute Ltd has financial market expertise garnered over three decades from its namesake and hence would be the perfect avenue for learning about financial markets. Another key highlight of this program is the 6 months industry engagement offered during the course as well as the 100% placement assistance after completion. The course provides opportunities to network with industry professionals, which can be beneficial while pursuing a career in financial markets.

The admission process for Global Financial Market Professional program is as under:

  • Fill the application form online and submit the required documents.
  • Appear for a personal interview round with BSE institute Ltd faculty.
  • Acknowledge and accept the offer letter on securing admission

The Global Financial Market Professional program can be pursued by students who have completed their HSC and above.

The Global Financial Market Professional program is conducted at BSE Institute Ltd campus in Delhi.

The Global Financial Market Course is a comprehensive vocational financial market program spanning across 2 years. This program can also be pursued in 1 year under the fastrack mode.
