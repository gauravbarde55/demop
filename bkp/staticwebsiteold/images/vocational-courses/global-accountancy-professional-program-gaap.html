About Global Accountancy Professional Program (GAPP)

Global Accountancy Professional Program (GAPP) is an online international accounting course offered by BSE Institute Ltd for students looking to understand the principles of accounting utilized in various business and organizational scenarios. The expertly designed curriculum of this accounting course is designed to help the students to develop cognizance of the importance of accounting through grass root level knowledge of accounting combined with case study based learning.

The Global Accountancy Professional Program is an online comprehensive course offered by BSE Institute Ltd for students seeking to forge a promising career in accounting. This program covers use of advanced accounting knowledge and techniques to address organizational issues, evaluation of complex accounting situations, assessment of global accounting issues that can contribute to organizational success and many more accounting procedures and principles.

We live in a time when start-ups are dealing with billions of dollars. In order to maintain track of such large sums of money, businesses are searching for skilled accounting professionals who are not only good at crunching numbers but also have a sharp business sense. Accountants work in a variety of fields, including business, trade, government, financial organisations, individuals and families, and others.

Accounting is in high demand, as it plays a vital role in controlling the economic impact of COVID-19 and assisting customers in adapting business models in uncertain and changing situations. With the expansion of various finance/accounting activities and technology, professionals are realising the breadth of opportunities that a foundation in accounting provides, leading to either a classic numbers-focused role or a more dynamic and business-focused career. An accounting certification like Global Accountancy Professional Program (GAPP) provides students with a highly valued, adaptable, and in-demand skill set, as well as demonstrating fluency in global accounting language. Accounting careers range from auditing, tax accounting, and cost accounting to managerial accounting, with many professional certifications available for each.

BSE Institute, as a pioneer and established leader in Financial Education, focuses on developing talent for the BFSI industry. The Global Accountancy Professional Program is the best program for someone seeking to forge a career in the financial accounting domain. And where else could one learn about finance, than the financial stomp ground of India itself - Bombay Stock Exchange Limited. BSE Institute Ltd has financial market expertise garnered over three decades from its namesake and hence would be the perfect avenue for learning about accounting. Another key highlight of this program is the 100% placement assistance after completion. The course provides opportunities to network with industry professionals, which can be beneficial while pursuing a career in accounting.
