About BBA - Banking and Financial Services Analytics

BBA in Banking & Financial Services Analytics, a graduate course offered by BSE Institute Ltd in collaboration with Homi Bhabha State University (HBSU), covers the study of basic banking administrative concepts with specialized focus on Financial Analysis.

The objective of this BBA course in Banking & Financial Services Analytics is to provide a common language for banking and finance by analysing and interpreting financial data as well as utilizing different financial tools to decipher market trends and convert them into profitable future business decisions.

The BBA - Banking and Financial Analytics integrates a state-of-the-art curriculum with industry experience. The academic curriculum covers all aspects of finance & is approved by Dr. Homi Bhabha State University. It begins with the very basics which form the core of finance, such as introduction to financial market and derivatives, fundamental analysis and reporting, Indian Banking structure & laws, Data visualization.

It eventually goes into highly advanced, specialized fields of study like Forex Markets, Financial Modelling, Algo-trading Corporate Banking Operations and Portfolio Management.

The BBA course Banking and Financial Services Analytics is a three-year undergraduate course pursued by students who have completed their 12th grade. The Banking and Financial Services Analytics course, imparts students with knowledge across a number of banking concepts and tools and how data analytics is applied to various banking and financial services, thereby getting them up and running to pursue a number of banking or financial data analytics careers or further studying options.

With the emergence and implementation of data analytics, financial services especially the banking industry has been able to optimise procedures and streamline operations, enhancing efficiency and competitiveness. Many financial sectors including banks are attempting to improve their data analytics, either to obtain a competitive advantage or to forecast upcoming trends that may influence their businesses. This has resulted in an increase in the demand for banking and financial professionals with data analytic skills.

Nowhere has data science and analytics been more effective in bringing about change than in banking and finance. The use of analytics is critical in financial services and other data-intensive industries. Because finance is a transaction-heavy industry, financial services organisations, especially investment banks, generate and maintain more data than just about any other business in any other sector. Due to this, career opportunities in Banking and Financial Services Analytics are emerging with every passing day, thus resulting in growing demand for data analytic expertise in the form of financial and banking professionals.
