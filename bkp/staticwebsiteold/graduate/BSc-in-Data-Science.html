About BSc in Data Science (Homi Bhabha)

BSc in Data Science is a three-year graduate program offered by BSE Institute Ltd in collaboration with Homi Bhabha State University. It is a well-known fact that data Science is a recent but rapidly growing field providing students with exciting career paths.

The objective of the BSc in Data Science course is to enhance data management skills in students to create qualified future professionals in the field of data science.

The BSc in Data Science integrates a state-of-the-art curriculum with industry experience. This program includes learning tools like Jupyter Notebook, which is an open-source web application that will be used by participants to create and share documents that contain live code in Python, R and other programming languages, equations and visualizations.

BSc Data Science is a three-year undergraduate course, focusing on various aspects of data science and big data analytics. This course is suitable for students who wish to build a lucrative career in the domain of data science.

After the completion of BSc Data Science students will get enormous employment opportunities in the domain like data science, business intelligence, machine learning and computer science. Data science professionals are one of the fastest-growing and highest-paid professionals in India and worldwide.

B.Sc. Data Science is one of the most popular programs, with the highest pay and job security available anywhere in the world. In order to comprehend a set of real-world data, the course covers Artificial Intelligence, Business Analytics, Statistics, Big Data Analytics, and other relevant fields.
The B.Sc. data science is the best course for someone seeking to forge a career in data science. With over 30 years experience in providing top notch expertise in finance, data science, securities, etc, BSE Institute Ltd. grooms young talent, trains them accordingly and prepares them for a successful career in their selected industry.

The Admission Process to enroll in the BSc Data Science course at BSE Institute Ltd are as follows-

  • Fill out the application form online at Click here or collect the form from our BSE Institute Limited in Kolkata.
  • Appear for the aptitude test, where your basic competency will be tested and gauged.
  • Get shortlisted for a personal interview round with our faculty.
  • Acknowledge the offer letter and confirm your admission

Eligibility Criteria for BSc Data Science course:

  • Students who have passed their HSC examination with a minimum of 60% from a recognized University
  • Mathematics/ Statistics subject in HSC with 50% aggregate marks
BSc Data Science course is an offline course and the lectures will be conducted at BSE Institute Ltd., Mumbai.
The duration of the BSc Data Science course is 3 years. With two semesters per year, the course is divided into 6 semesters.
